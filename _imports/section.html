LUXURY OUTERWEAR FOR MEN SINCE 1911

With more than a century of experience in upholding the Made in Italy tradition of true craftsmanship, LuxCraft is a contemporary luxury house founded in 1911 in Milan. Renowned for its signature creation, the CraftMaster jacket, each seasonal collection consists of a wide range of garments suited for every occasion using soft suedes, technical fabrics, and woven materials by the hands of expert craftsmen with years of know-how in crafting understated luxury.
